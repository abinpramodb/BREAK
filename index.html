Welcome to Take a Break
Step 1 of 6
Take a Break is a strategy card game of skill, timing, and disruption. You must collect all 4 cards of one family sequence and defend your winning position for one full round — while opponents try to break you.
⭐
Hold to Win
Complete a 4-card sequence and survive one full round of attacks
💀
Last Standing
Eliminate all opponents by draining their cards through power moves
💡 Tip: The game has 55 cards total — 36 character cards and 19 power cards. You start with 5 cards and draw 1 each turn.
There are 3 families, each with 4 ranked cards. Place character cards from your hand onto your Field (visible to all). Complete any one family to declare a win!

💡 Tip: You can only place each rank once per family. Focus on the family where you already have the most cards!
Every turn follows the same simple steps:
1
Draw — Automatically draw 1 card from the deck at the start of your turn.
2
Select — Click a card in your hand to select it (it will lift and glow gold).
3
Play or Discard — Use the action buttons at the bottom:
• Play to Field — places a character card on your field
• Activate Power — uses a power card's special effect
• Discard — removes the card without effect
4
Win Check — If your field now has all 4 cards of one family, you enter WIN PENDING state!
Power cards create chaos and shift control. Select one and click Activate Power:
🎒
Thief (1 / 2 / 3 Sacks)
Steal 1, 2, or 3 random cards from any player's hand. Great for weakening opponents or grabbing cards you need.
🔄
Swap
Exchange your hand with another player, OR swap two other players' hands with each other. Fields are not affected.
💥
BREAK
The most powerful card! The target loses ALL field cards and their entire hand, then redraws 5 cards. Cancels win pending!
🛡️
Power Swap (Defensive)
Cannot be played offensively. When you are attacked, a prompt appears — use it to redirect the attack to any player!
When you complete a 4-card sequence, you enter WIN PENDING mode:
1
A golden banner appears at the top: "⚔️ WIN PENDING — N turn(s) remaining"
2
Every other active player gets one turn to try to stop you using Break, Thief, or Swap.
3
If your sequence is still intact after all opponents have played → YOU WIN! 🏆
4
If a Break clears your field, your win is cancelled and the game continues.
💡 Strategy: Save your Power Swap card for when someone tries to Break you during your win pending round!
Two special cards are awarded as prizes — not part of the deck:
🐒
Monkey Card
Awarded to the first player eliminated. They must survive the next game to shed it. A badge of shame — avoid being eliminated first!
👑
Break Supremacy
Awarded to the winner. In the next game, the holder goes first — a powerful strategic advantage!
🎯 You're ready to play! Remember: draw → select a card → play to field or activate a power. Complete any 4-card family to claim victory!
